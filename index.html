Welcome to Helpers' Hub! Our team recognizes the gap of ammenities low-income communities often face and want to combat that by building recreation/community centers in these cities. We believe everyone should have free access to athletics, education, and artistic creativity. For that reason, we pledge to keep memberships, events, and resources low-cost, if not free.
